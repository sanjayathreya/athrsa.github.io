Bitcoin boom-bust cycles

2012 drawdown All Time high

The first crash of bitcoin took place in 2012

  • In February of 2011, BTC reached $1.00
  • Bitcoin had risen 100x to $30 from starting price of about $0.30.
  • By end of year, the price of Bitcoin was under $5-no one can say why
  • Litecoin, a fork of the Bitcoin blockchain was launched
  • In 2012, of course, Bitcoin saw its first halving
  • From a 50-coin reward for mining BTC to 25 coins.
  • Set the stage for incredible growth

2016 drawdown All Time high

In 2013, bitcoin had a decisive bull run ending December at over $1000 over 1000x.

  • Cryptocurrencies quietly expanded in this period.
  • In terms of price bitcoin plunged to $200.
  • It held steady there between $200 and $400.
  • Proof of stake mining emerged as an alternative to Proof of work.
  • One year after having, a retail frenzy propelled BTC

2018 drawdown All Time high

BTC went mainstream in 2017 drawing a lot of coverage.

  • BTC touched $20,000 in late 2017
  • A lot of variying opinions started being discussed.
  • It was the greatest thing ever or the greatest scam.
  • This period also saw the rise of multiple alt-coins.
  • Soon after the parabolic rise, emerged another crypto winter in 2018-2019.

2020 drawdown All Time high

The third bitcoin halving and pandemic saw an incredible rally.

  • With millions at home, online speculation grew in popularity.
  • Meme investing along with celebrity tweeting such is Elon musk egged on rally.
  • Bitcoin saw another meteoric rise in 2020 to over $60,000 in 2021.
  • While it felt like bubble popped in 2021, BTC made a epic comeback in 45 days.

Today

Inflationary fears and Fed rate hikes saw a >50% collapse again in BTC.

  • Toward end of 2021, BTC hashrate factor plummeted.
  • China partly shutdown its mining operations due to power consumption.
  • This period saw a massive rally in commodity and energy sector.
  • As Fed stoked inflationry fears, tech sector and speculative assets sold off.
  • As of July 24,2022 BTC was priced around $20,000.

Will bitcoin go to the moon? Remains to be seen...
